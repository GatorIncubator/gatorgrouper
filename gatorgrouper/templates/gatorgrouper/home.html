Developer Story

Professors and students alike at Allegheny College have previously struggled with sucessfully creating functional student groups, while maintaining productivity.

Group organization is often a challenging experience that frequently results in conflicts among students, unproductive group members, as well as loss of time.

The Software Engineering team at Allegheny College eventually determined that current grouping methods are ineffective and sought to develop a program that would allow for productive, functional and effortless group organization.

Product Desrcription

GatorGrouper is a tool designed for teachers and project managers to organize classrooms or large teams into groups. The grouping method takes in to account individual skill level and user feedback on group creation to keep administrators in-the-loop.

Users of GatorGrouper can login with a Google account to be placed into groups with other users from the same project.

GatorGrouper aims to create balanced and productive groups to facilitate a healthy and productive work environment. Each user is given a survey before group assignment. Subsequent groupings are influenced by a user review of their group experience.
